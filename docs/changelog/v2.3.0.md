## Summary

Two independent reliability improvements:

1. **TX retry-drop counter (`radio_retry_drops`)** â€” `RadioDispatcher::enqueueRetry()` now
   maintains a saturating `retryDrops_` counter that increments each time the TX retry buffer
   (`kMaxRetrySlots = 4`) is full and a reliable frame must be sent best-effort.  The counter
   is exposed via a new public getter `retryDrops()` and surfaced in `GET /api/status` under
   `health.radio_retry_drops`.  A non-zero and growing value is a ground-station-detectable
   signal of link degradation (APUS-4.5).

2. **TWDT sleep cap** â€” `loop()` previously computed a sleep duration of up to 60 s from the
   AMS wakeup schedule and passed it directly to `vTaskDelay()`.  With
   `CONFIG_ESP_TASK_WDT_TIMEOUT_S = 5 s`, any sleep exceeding ~2.5 s would miss the
   `esp_task_wdt_reset()` at the top of the next iteration and trigger a panic.  A new
   constant `LOOP_SLEEP_MAX_MS = 2000U` (< half the 5 s timeout) caps every delay.
